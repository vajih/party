How to Join a Party
1
Get the Party Link
Ask the party host to send you the party link. It will look something like:
yourpartyapp.com/?party=amazing-party
2
Click the Link
Open the link in your web browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, etc.). You'll see a page asking for your email.
3
Enter Your Email
Type in your email address and click "Email me a magic link". We'll send you a special login link - no password needed!
4
Check Your Email
Look for our email (check your spam folder too!). Click the link in the email to instantly join the party.

Are you a host?

Quick Tips

We use "magic links" instead of passwords - just click the link we email you to sign in securely!
If you're having trouble, ask the party host for help or check that you're using the correct party link.
