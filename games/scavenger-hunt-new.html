Dilwale Scavenger Hunt

Conversations Over Checkmarks

Guidance

  • Connect, don't compete: This activity is a catalyst for real conversations at your table.
  • How: At your table, pick prompts, ask & listen to 10–15 second stories; initials optional—quality over quantity.
  • Then share: After ~15 minutes, each of the 12 tables offers one standout story to the room.
